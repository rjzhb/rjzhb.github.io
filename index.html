Biography

I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. Kexin Rong. I also closely collaborate with Prof. Ada Gavrilovska and Prof. Steve Mussmann. Previously, I was a research intern at Columbia University, working with Prof. Eugene Wu. Additionally, I have many industry engineering experience, which you can learn more about on my LinkedIn.

Overall, my research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and data-intensive systems/tools, focusing on building efficient, scalable, and intelligent solutions, including but not limited to:

  • Data-Centric AI (e.g., Data Exploration)
  • RAG Systems(e.g., Vector Database Systems, Cache Systems)
  • Data Systems for Machine Learning (e.g., Model Selection Systems)
  • Data Analytics Systems (e.g., Spark, Flink, Video/Image Analytics)
